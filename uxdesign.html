What is the course about ?

This is an 8-week course for people who want to learn end to end UX design by following the design thinking process and build a strong UX portfolio. By the end of this course, the participant will have a complete UX project case study in their portfolio and the working capability of handling any project from scratch using various UX methodologies. What will you learn?

Diving deep into UI/ UX design and the Design Thinking process. Learn how to solve problems from scratch and creating great User Experiences, finish a live project and get hands-on experience working with professionals. Who is eligible?

Experienced professionals such as analysts, engineers, programmers, managers, artists, writers, entrepreneurs and designers can take up this course. Students or professionals from any stream engineering, arts or commerce are welcome to join. Who is going to train you?

Trainers are graduates and postgraduates in design from NID and IIT with extensive experience in the UX Industry with companies such as Microsoft, Samsung, HFI, AJIO. They have worked in a variety of domains, creating experiences in IOT, Virtual reality, Augmented reality, Physical Computing, Web and Mobile.
What is the avg package after you complete the course?

Packages anything from 6lpa to 35lpa are offered by companies. What are the placement opportunities?

Your portfolio will be circulated to our clients and other companies. You should be able to get atleast 3 interview calls. With a good portfolio, cracking an interview is easy.
